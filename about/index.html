Co-Founders

Rasmus Tauts • Ian Leman

We are taking business contact quality to the next level!

The founders of ContactPit, Rasmus and Ian, believe that searching for business contacts should be efficient and easily accessible to everyone. Ian's extensive experience in the marketing landscape proves that high-quality contact lists play a very important role in email marketing. Contacts are the core of an email campaign - no matter how interesting your offer may be, if the email addresses are not personal and valid, the campaign will not yield the desired results.

We have repeatedly seen how marketing campaigns send emails to general business addresses (info@) or outdated email addresses. The combination of Ian's marketing knowledge and Rasmus's IT skills led to the development of the ContactPit platform.

Where does ContactPit source its business contacts from?

ContactPit's data originates from the Estonian Business Registry. In the Business Registry, it is possible to make queries about all legal entities, but queries can only be made one at a time. From ContactPit's database, companies can be differentiated based on filters, and suitable contacts can be downloaded all at once. We update the information in the ContactPit database daily.

In addition, we verify all email addresses to ensure that emails can be securely sent to the downloaded contacts. During verification, we remove all faulty and outdated contacts, so that only functional email addresses remain.
